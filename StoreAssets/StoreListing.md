## 英语(美国) - English (United States)

### 应用名称
QuickWheel - Quick Launcher

### 简短描述 (Short Description, 500字符以内)
A fast circular menu launcher for Windows. Hold hotkey, move mouse to select, release to execute. Boost your productivity with customizable shortcuts.

### 详细描述 (Description)
QuickWheel is a powerful and intuitive quick launcher for Windows that helps you access your favorite apps, files, folders, and commands with lightning speed.

**Key Features:**

🎯 **Circular Quick Menu** 
Hold your configured hotkey (default: Ctrl+Alt+Q) to instantly bring up a circular menu. Simply move your mouse to the desired sector and release the hotkey to execute. No clicking required!

⚡ **Multiple Action Types**
- Open files and applications
- Open folders instantly
- Launch URLs in your browser
- Execute custom commands with parameters
- Send keyboard shortcuts

📝 **Template Variables**
Supercharge your shortcuts with dynamic variables:
- `{Clipboard}` - Insert clipboard content
- `{SelectedText}` - Use currently selected text
- `{Date}` - Current date (yyyy-MM-dd)
- `{Time}` - Current time (HH:mm:ss)

**Example:** Create a shortcut that searches Google for your clipboard content:
- Command: `cmd`
- Parameters: `/c start https://google.com/search?q={Clipboard}`

🎨 **Highly Customizable**
- Personalize colors and appearance
- Choose from a wide range of icons
- Configure your preferred hotkey
- Drag and drop to reorder shortcuts

🌐 **Multilingual Support**
Available in English and Chinese (Simplified).

🔔 **Optional Notifications**
Get notified when actions are executed.

**Perfect for:**
- Power users who want quick access to tools
- Developers needing fast command execution
- Anyone looking to boost their Windows productivity

Download QuickWheel today and experience a faster way to work!
